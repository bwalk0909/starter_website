  • People shop on iPad's dammit! Support that browser!

Using min and max widths

min and max-widths are complimentary to percentage based design

The idea is simple, sometimes content needs to always be at least a minimum width, and sometimes it shouldn't grow beyond a certain width, and sometimes both apply

my min-width is 600px! I have a width of 100% but I won't shrink below 600px
I'll never go above 450px! try as you might, but I can go less!
I have both a min and max width!
